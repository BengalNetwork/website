Bengal Network

We aim to help rescue Bengal kitties who are abandonded, unwanted, abused or in situations that are not good for their survival. We support different Bengal Rescue and Adoption Agencie throughout North and South America. A lot of Bengal Kitties end up without a home because unsuspecting owners have no idea what they are getting themselves into. Bengals have high energy and need a lot of attention. If they feel neglected they will let you know. A lot of these owners end up abandoning their kitties or giving them up, we are here to help those Bengals find homes.
