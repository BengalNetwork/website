OUR MISSION

Saving Bengal Kitties by making donations to legitimate Bengal cat rescue and adoption agencies throughout North and South America.

What is a Bengal?

A hybrid cat that comes from breeding Asian Leopard Cats and domestic house cats. Bengals have a wild appearance with various different patterns

Bengal Temperments

An energentic breed which require a lot of attention. Bengals are very smart and playful, they can be trained to fetch toys and walk on a leash. Bengals get into a lot of mischief.

Bengal Adoption

Bengal's will cost you anywhere from $1200 to about $5000. A widely popular house cat with a friendly nature and super affectionate attitude.

History

The Bengal Kitty history can be traced back to 1965 when an female Asian Leopard cat was mated with a domestic male cat and produced a littler of Bengal Offspring.

The Negatives

Bengals have a lot of energy and require a lot of attention. If you neglect your Bengal they will become sad and anxious. Sometimes they will urinate around your living spaces.

Best Practices

You need to do a lot of research before you decide to adopt a Bengal Kitty. They are very mischievous and termpermental, not for every cat lover out there.
